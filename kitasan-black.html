Kitasan Black

Character Profile

A cheerful and friendly Uma Musume who loves festivals and bringing smiles to everyone's faces. Kitasan Black is known for her immense popularity and her powerful, crowd-pleasing running style.

She is a symbol of hard work and optimism, always striving to be a hero for her fans. Her signature cry, "Wasshoi!", echoes her festive spirit and her desire to share joy and victory with the world.
